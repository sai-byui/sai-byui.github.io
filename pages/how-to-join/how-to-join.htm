How to Join

Welcome!

The Society of Artificial Intelligence at BYU - Idaho welcomes any student who wants to learn about artificial intelligence, machine learning, or programming.

To keep up to date with the Society, see our page at I-Belong

Where Does the Society Meet?

Meetings are held over Microsoft Teams each Thursday at 6. Meetings start the third week of the semester and end a week before the semester ends.

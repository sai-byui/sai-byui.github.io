About Us

The Society of Artificial Intelligence welcomes any student who is seeking to develop leadership, teambuilding, and techinical skills such as programming, version control, and machine learning algorithms while exploring the field of Artificial Intelligence.
Meetings are held weekly in-person on Thursdays at 6pm MST and are streamed on Microsoft Teams during semesters! Click on the meeting post in the General channel. Many society projects are available on the GitHub Respository.
